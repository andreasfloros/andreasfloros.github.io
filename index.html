About me

I’m an Electrical Electronic Engineering graduate from Imperial College London. My interests lie in the fields of Machine Learning and Signal Processing as well as Software Development. Some of my favourite franchises are Final Fantasy and the Xenoblade series. Aside from music and games, I enjoy the occasional book.
